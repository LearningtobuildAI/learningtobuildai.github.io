Construction Automation JobsNOW

What the job is: Site surveying, quantity takeoff, safety monitoring, progress tracking.
What tasks it does: Repeated measuring, reporting, scheduling, materials checks.
How automation works: Vision systems, digital twins, robotic layout tools, RPA reports.
Materials or tech needed: Drones (civil), scanners, BIM stack, autonomous equipment controls.
Process to automate it: Map workflow, digitize inputs, deploy assistive robotics, validate outputs.
Energy required: Medium for scanning, compute, and robotic equipment support.

Healthcare and Retail Automation JobsNOW

What the job is: Triage support, claims processing, inventory and checkout operations.
What tasks it does: Data entry, queue management, restocking, billing workflows.
How automation works: Clinical decision support, RPA back office, shelf vision, smart POS.
Materials or tech needed: EHR APIs, robotics carts, scanners, workflow engines.
Process to automate it: Standardize SOPs, integrate systems, run supervised pilots, scale safely.
Energy required: Low to medium with continuous compute and device charging.

Logistics and Manufacturing Automation JobsNOW

What the job is: Picking, packing, QA inspection, line balancing, dispatch planning.
What tasks it does: Repetitive handling, quality checks, route scheduling, exception triage.
How automation works: AMRs, robotic cells, OCR, predictive optimization, digital control towers.
Materials or tech needed: Conveyors, sensors, industrial robots, orchestration software.
Process to automate it: Baseline KPIs, automate high-volume loops, fail-safe and rollback testing.
Energy required: Medium to high depending on throughput and robotics density.

Services, IT, and Public Sector Automation JobsNOW

What the job is: Helpdesk, compliance, case management, finance and policy operations.
What tasks it does: Ticket routing, document validation, reconciliation, reporting.
How automation works: RPA, LLM copilots, workflow automation, anomaly detection.
Materials or tech needed: Secure APIs, IAM controls, audit logs, governance tooling.
Process to automate it: Classify tasks, apply low-risk automation first, monitor human override paths.
Energy required: Low to medium, primarily data-center and endpoint compute.

Safety Rule (Mandatory)

Forbidden: military technology, defense systems, combat drones, missiles, targeting systems, autonomous weapons, and any new weapon concepts. Allowed: cybersecurity, civil robotics, industrial automation, medical technology, energy systems, materials science, space technology, construction technology.
