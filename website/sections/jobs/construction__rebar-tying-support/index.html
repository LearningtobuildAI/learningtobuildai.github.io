Rebar Tying Support

Risk: Low AI 80% Robot 65% Full auto ~2030

The 'Rebar Tying Support' job involves manually attaching rebar to formwork structures in construction sites. This is a repetitive and physically demanding task.

1 · What the Job Does

  • Identify the correct rebar to tie.
  • Locate the precise point where the rebar needs to be tied.
  • Use ties (like wire or plastic ties) to connect the rebar securely to the formwork.
  • Ensure all ties are tightly fastened to maintain structural integrity.
  • Repeat for all required sections of the formwork.

2 · AI Replacement Pathway

AI programs: Machine Vision AI for precise object detection and alignment, Reinforcement Learning algorithms for optimal tie application, Predictive Maintenance AI for rebar handling equipment

Timeline: AI systems can be implemented within 2-3 years, with continuous improvements every year.

3 · Robot Replacement Pathway

Robot type: Mobile robotic arms equipped with advanced grippers and vision sensors

Timeline: Robots could be deployed in large-scale construction projects within 5-7 years after initial development.
