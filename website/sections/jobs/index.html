How a Job Gets Replaced — Step by Step

Automation almost always follows the same five stages. A role rarely vanishes overnight — it's hollowed out one stage at a time, and humans move up the chain toward judgement, exceptions and oversight.

STAGE 1
Digitisation
The work moves from paper/manual into digital systems and data.
STAGE 2
Task Automation
Repetitive, rule-based steps are scripted away (RPA, macros).
STAGE 3
AI Prediction
Models learn patterns and start suggesting the next action.
STAGE 4
AI Decisions
The system acts on its own for the common cases, unattended.
STAGE 5
Full Autonomy
Humans only handle exceptions, oversight and the human touch.

The higher up this chain you work — judgement, relationships, exceptions, physical craft — the safer your role.
