RAG, MoE & On-Device AI ArchitectureNEAR

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) grounds LLMs in live knowledge bases, eliminating hallucinations for enterprise use. Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) models like Mixtral and GPT-4o selectively activate only relevant parameters, reducing inference cost 4-8x. On-device AI (Apple Intelligence, Gemini Nano, Phi-3 Mini) runs models locally - enabling private, latency-free AI on 2B+ smartphones by 2026. These three architectures define the infrastructure layer of practical AI deployment.

Artificial General Intelligence TransitionFAR

AGI - systems matching or exceeding human cognitive performance across all economically valuable tasks - is projected by leading researchers at 2027-2035 depending on definition and metric. The transition is not expected to be a single moment but a capability gradient where AI systems progressively dominate professional domains. The post-AGI economy involves recursive self-improvement cycles, where AI systems design their own next-generation architectures, compressing decades of R&D into years.

OPENAI · ANTHROPIC · DEEPMIND · FUTURE OF HUMANITY INSTITUTE
Neuromorphic and Quantum AIFAR

Beyond transformer architectures, neuromorphic chips (Intel Loihi, IBM NorthPole) mimic biological neural firing patterns with orders-of-magnitude energy efficiency gains. Quantum machine learning remains speculative at scale but offers theoretical exponential speedups for specific optimisation and sampling problems. The convergence of quantum computing with neural architectures could enable AI systems operating on fundamentally different computational substrates by mid-century.
